Wedding Timeline

A detailed schedule of our two-day celebration filled with traditions, ceremonies, and joyous moments.

November 24th

Arrival & Lunch

Welcome to our celebration! Guests arrive and enjoy a traditional lunch featuring Gujarati specialties and regional delicacies. The festivities begin with warm hospitality.

3:00 PM - 5:00 PM

Ganesh & Pithi Ceremony

Sacred rituals begin with Ganesh Puja followed by the vibrant Pithi ceremony. A time of blessings, turmeric applications, and joyful family traditions.

4:00 PM - 6:00 PM

Hi-Tea Service

Relax and mingle with delicious snacks, chaats, and refreshing beverages. Perfect time to connect with family and friends.

7:00 PM - 10:30 PM

Sangeet & Dinner

The night comes alive with music, dance, and performances! Enjoy a lavish dinner with live counters, international cuisines, and endless entertainment.

November 25th - 7:00 AM

Gotraj Pooja & Pallu

Dawn breaks with sacred ceremonies. Traditional rituals marking the beginning of the wedding day with family blessings and prayers.

8:00 AM - 10:00 AM

Wedding Breakfast

Start the special day with an elaborate breakfast featuring South Indian delicacies, Gujarati farsan, fresh juices, and traditional sweets.

11:34 AM

Wedding Muhurat

The most sacred moment! Witness the beautiful wedding ceremony as Yash and Soniya take their vows in the auspicious muhurat time.

5:49 PM

Sunset Varmala

A magical moment as the couple exchanges garlands during the golden hour. Capture this beautiful ritual against the sunset backdrop.

8:00 PM - 9:00 PM

Bidai Ceremony

An emotional and beautiful farewell as the bride begins her new journey. A traditional ceremony filled with love, tears, and blessings.
